hub-WOW

What is this?

hub-WOW is a community of people who are passionate about memes, science, politics, philosophy, hardware, programming, and much more. Our goal is to spark the inner potential available in everyone and direct it towards making a better world through meaningful discussions, sharing knowledge, and inspiring others.

Long-Term Vision

  • Build a global network of thinkers, revolutionaries, and doers.
  • Launch collaborative projects that have real-world impact.
  • Inspire individuals to take charge of their potential and drive positive change.
  • Develop educational resources, workshops, and events to empower everyone, everywhere.

Our Mission

  • Awaken the mind, body, and soul and channelise it towards a purpose.
  • Connect people with interests and expertise, to form a uniques space, where ideas come together and form a vision.
  • Ideate and discuss about various fields, to build a mental compass of ethics to navigate through life.
  • Innovate by encouraging creative solutions and open discussions, without any judgement or censorship.
  • Contribute by sharing your knowledge, projects, and ideas, and help others grow too.

Community Values

  • Ethics - Nothing is worth without a string mind. Morals limit you physically, but they teach you mental restraint. Instead of falling to primal senses and attractions of greed, lust, anger, jealousy, and hatred, and accepting "free" corporate gunk, we build a free transparent system.
  • Respect & Empathy – Every opinion matters; every member deserves a chance to express their opinion with dignity and freedom.
  • Curiosity & Growth – Always question, break mental barriers and stigmas, learn, progress, act and evolve.
  • Collaboration & Support – Success matters only when you leave a mark of excellent legacy on this world, and that is only possible with collaboration and support.
  • Integrity & Accountability – Take ownership of your actions, sufferings, stupidity, biases, limitations and try to continuously out manuevre them.
  • Fun & Creativity – Having a purpose is more important than fulfilling it. Striving towards a goal is what matters. It is always fun when you make it, but it is a side-effect of all your effort.

Core Philosophy & Economics

  • Untapped Potential – We deeply believe that everyone has unimaginable potential, but they simply don't take action. Society lacks the critical self-awareness needed to truly unlock its own capabilities.
  • Balanced Capitalism – We believe in capitalism as a tool to achieve a balanced socialist society where everyone feels like contributing. A society where capitalism is good, fostering innovation rather than exploitation.
  • Radical Transparency – Companies must be fully transparent (exceptions apply for defense). Highly profitable tech companies with 50-60% margins should open their books, capping excess to realistic 6-7% margins.
  • Open Knowledge & Tech – Every idea and piece of knowledge must be free to access. We stand firmly against the abuse of patent rights which stifle true technological and scientific revolution.
  • Critical Nationalism – We support critical nationalism, not blind patriotism. We support absolute criticism of any kind, questioning everything to ensure constant iterative improvement for the nation and humanity.
  • Passive Protest & Open Source – We believe in protesting passively by refusing to use certain products. We encourage keeping all work aggressively open source, licensing everything strictly to protect freedom (e.g., under AGPL GNU v3).

Why Join?

  • Engage in open, free, and uncensored discussions on a variety of topics.
  • Develop an internal sense of responsibility, judgement, and discipline to act towards excellence.
  • Share your own projects, ideas, and discoveries.
  • Collaborate with a supportive and motivated people, who just as you are, yet perform and achieve high.
  • Stay updated on the latest trends and views in technology, science, history and culture.

Get Committed

  1. Join our community platforms on Discord and our website.
  2. Participate in discussions and events.
  3. Share content, projects, and resources.
  4. Help build a community where ideas sprout and mature.
  5. Track and evaluate yourself daily, and progress.

hub-WOW is more than a community, it’s a movement towards a better, free, open and progressive world.
